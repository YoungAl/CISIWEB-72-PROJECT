Cleaning Tips

General Cleaning Tips

At SoCal General Cleaning Services, we care about our clients and want to provide them with the best possible cleaning experience. That's why we not only offer top-notch cleaning services, but we also provide cleaning tips to help our clients maintain a clean and healthy living or working environment between our visits. We believe that sharing our expertise and knowledge can help our clients take better care of their homes or businesses, and we're always looking for ways to improve our services and offer more value to our clients.

Tips:

  • Clean your shower head with vinegar.
  • Clean your microwave with lemon juice.
  • Clean your windows with newspaper.
  • Clean your carpets with baking soda.

Safety

Safety is of utmost importance when it comes to cleaning. Cleaning products and equipment can pose significant health hazards if used improperly, and it is important to take precautions to avoid accidents or injury. In addition to the potential risks to our own health and safety, cleaning products can also have harmful effects on the environment if not disposed of properly. At SoCal General Cleaning Services, we take safety seriously and make every effort to ensure that our clients and our team are protected while cleaning. We use environmentally-friendly cleaning products whenever possible, and we take care to properly dispose of any hazardous materials. By prioritizing safety in all of our cleaning practices, we are able to provide our clients with a healthy and clean living or working environment, while minimizing any potential risks or harm. For more information, please visit the official government website for cleaning safety here: Osha Website
